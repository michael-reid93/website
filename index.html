Home Deliveries

Groceries and essentials, at your door.

Stuck in quarantine? Can't leave your house? We'll do the shopping for you and deliver it right to your door steps. The stay at home care pack includes various canned goods, one horse apple, one small block of clay, one crossword puzzle, and one roll of premium 3-ply toilet paper. Starting from just $120 per month.

Zoom Fitness Classes

Stay fit and healthy this quarantine.

We hold daily Zoom yoga and dance classes so that you can stay physically and mentally healthy during mandatory isolation. Starting from just $60 per lesson.

Research

We'll stop Covid-19 for good.

We're working with top universities and organisations across the country to research and gather data so that we can stop this deadly virus for good. Please donate to help a good cause for all of us.
