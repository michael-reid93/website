About Us

We are MISC. The Melbourne Initiative to Stop Covid-19. Our goal is simple; to help those who have been affected by the virus, and to work together with organisations to end the pandemic as soon as we can. Our services include home delivery of groceries and essentials, hosting Zoom yoga and dance classes and assisting organisations with research.
